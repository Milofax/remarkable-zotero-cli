# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be useful, calm, and safe to contribute to. Contributors
and maintainers are expected to treat each other with respect, patience, and
good faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume honest mistakes before bad intent.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect privacy and copyright boundaries around sample documents.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private or copyrighted documents without permission.
- Publishing security details that put users at risk before maintainers can
  respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations
may lead to a ban from participating in the project.
